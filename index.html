Interesting Facts about Chameleons

Chameleons have eyes in the backs of their heads

Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues

Chameleons take a while to hatch, and don’t live very long

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

They Can Be Part of a Large Communication System

Man is a Pigeon’s Main Predator

Interesting Facts about Cicadas

They are loud, rivaling construction equipment and motorcycles.

Some cicadas could explode with fungus.

Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.

An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

They have great camouflage for their environment.

Interesting Facts about Camels

There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

They can withstand major temperature changes from the sweltering heat of the day to the cold desert nights.

Camels can survive up to 15 days without water.
